
Find the GCD of the two number X and Y.

gcd(X, Y, G) :- X = Y, G = X. 
gcd(X, Y, G) :- 
  X < Y, 
  Y1 is Y – X, 
  gcd(X, Y1, G). 
gcd(X, Y, G) :- X > Y, gcd(Y, X, G). 

Input:    gcd(10,5,G).

Output:  G = 5

Find the LCM of the two number X and Y.

gcd(X, Y, G) :- X = Y, G = X. 
gcd(X, Y, G) :- 
  X < Y, 
  Y1 is Y – X, 
  gcd(X, Y1, G). 
gcd(X, Y, G) :- X > Y, gcd(Y, X, G).

lcm(X,Y,LCM):-gcd(X,Y,GCD), LCM is X*Y//GCD. 

Input:   lcm(4,5,M).

Output: M = 20
